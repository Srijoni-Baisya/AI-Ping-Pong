AI Ping-Pong Game







Instructions

  1. Keep the laptop screen straight and move 3-4 feet away from the screen.
  2. When you move your right wrist up and down, a red dot will appear on your wrist.
  3. Press the Play button to start the game.
  4. You have to control the red paddle by moving your right wrist upwards and downwards. The yellow paddle will be controlled by the computer.
  5. You have to prevent the ball from touching the black-coloured side by controlling the red paddle, else the computer will gain points.
  6. When the game is over, press the Restart button to play again.
